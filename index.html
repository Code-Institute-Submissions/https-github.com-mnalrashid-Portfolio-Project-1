Beautiful Sligo

Sligo is known for its rugged coastal beauty. It is most scenic county with mountains and beaches.

Mountains

Sligo's majestic mountains are just breathtaking. From Benbulben, Ireland's table top mountain to the quiet Ox Mountains, it is time to discover these fabulous mountain ranges. Discover more about Sligo's mountains by clicking the pictures.

West Atlantic Ways

Sligo located on the Surf Coast of Ireland's Wild Atlantic Way, With over 190km of coastline and some of the most beautiful, unspoiled beaches in Ireland, Sligo is the perfect location to experience a costal walk. Discover more about Sligo's beautiful beaches by clicking the pictures
